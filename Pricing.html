Frequently asked questions

Is it easy to change plans?

It sure is. You can upgrade, downgrade, or switch from monthly to annual billing at any time.

I tried Podia and it wasn't for me. Can I have a refund?

We offer a free trial so that you can take Podia for a spin! If you sign up for a paid plan and would like a refund, please contact us within 72 hours after your monthly (or annual) renewal or upgrade, and we’ll be happy to refund the most recent payment.

Note: we do not offer pro-rated refunds for annual payments. Refunds take 5-10 business days to process.

If you’d like to cancel your plan, you can do so at any time. Just login to your Podia account, go to “account settings” and visit the “billing” section to cancel.

Are there any transaction fees or payment processing fees?

On the Mover plan, we charge a 5% transaction fee. There are no Podia fees on the Shaker plan.

Your payment processor (Stripe or PayPal) will charge a standard transaction fee of 2.9% + 30¢, which may vary based on your location. The fees are charged by the processor and are not associated with Podia.

Can I buy Podia Email without buying a Mover or Shaker plan?

You need a Mover or Shaker plan to use Podia Email beyond your 30-day free trial. All Podia plans and the free trial come with Podia Email free for up to 100 subscribers. After your free trial ends, you'll need to pick either a Mover or Shaker plan to continue using Podia Email, and you can always add on more subscribers as your list grows.

What happens when I reach the subscriber limit of my Podia Email plan?

You will receive a notification email as you approach the maximum number of subscribers for your plan. Once the limit is reached, you will be able to upgrade to the next plan tier for more subscribers. You will never be upgraded automatically, and you can downgrade to a lower plan tier whenever you want!

How do product and course migrations work?

The number of products we can migrate depends on your plan. - Mover: 20 products - Shaker: 30 products Here's what we can migrate for you: -Online course content, including files and text -Digital downloads, including files and text -All online course and digital download customer email addresses -All free community/membership subscribers

Can I pay you to build my website for me?

While we don’t offer an official website-building service, we can recommend people who do. The Podia Pros are expert designers, specialists, and educators who know Podia inside and out.

Whether you need a done-for-you service or some fresh new ideas, the Podia Pros are here to help with web design services, admin assistance, course and product setup, and consultations. Learn more about hiring a Podia Pro here.
