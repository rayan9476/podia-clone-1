Frequently asked questions

Can I use Podia Email forms on my other website?

You can embed your Podia Email sign-up form onto a third-party website like WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, or Weebly.

Can I make lead magnets in Podia?

You can set up lead magnets in Podia by adding your digital download and setting the price to “Free email delivery.” You can make as many lead magnets and landing pages as you like.

Can I build landing pages in Podia?

Absolutely! You can use the Podia website tool to create a full website, stand-alone landing pages, or both. You can also decide whether or not each page has a site header. No matter what you create, you’ll have access to the same great library of website sections and design features.

Does Podia have tagging and segmentation?

Podia automatically tracks purchases, sales, form subscriptions, and email activity so you can segment and filter your audience. You can also add custom tags to your audience members and create unique segments based on the information in your account.

Can I make my own templates with Podia Email?

Yep, you can. Podia comes with over a dozen premade email templates, but you can also set up your own template if you prefer. Create a design you like and it will be available in your “previous emails” tab. Click on it to apply the same style settings to a new message and you’ll be good to go.

Does Podia have sales funnels?

Podia makes it easy to build sales funnels since your products, landing pages, email list, and website are all connected. Podia Email has automations and campaigns so you can send messages to customers based on the actions they take in your business.

You can set up tags, segments, and entrance and exit conditions to run funnels for your products, and all email features are free for your first 100 subscribers.
