We'll move all your stuff to Podia for free

All of our Mover and Shaker plans come with free migrations. Our team will manually move your products, courses, email automations, and email list over to Podia for you. Here's how it works.

Learn more about free migrations
  • 1

    Sign up for any Mover or Shaker plan

    Product migrations are free with all paid plans (any monthly or annual Mover or Shaker plan). Email migrations are free with any paid Podia Email plan.

  • 2

    Request your migration

    We’ll ask you some questions about where you’re migrating from, and the details you use to access that platform, along with what, exactly, you want us to migrate. You can fill out the request form here.

  • 3

    Sign the agreement

    You’ll sign a short migration agreement outlining our migration terms. No scary legal jargon here, just a simple document explaining what we can and can’t migrate and how we’ll work to make this happen for you.

  • 4

    Sit back and relax

    When you’ve completed request form and signed the agreement, we’ll queue your migration for processing. We’ll give you a time estimate once we get started and let you know the second it’s done. (Note: Because we take extra care to manually migrate your data, we can’t offer refunds on any payments made prior to migrations being started.)

Frequently asked questions

What features does Podia have?

Podia is an all-in-one platform for running your online business. You get a full website, your own blog, unlimited landing pages, and the ability to sell digital products like courses, coaching, downloads, webinars, and any other type of file you like. Podia also includes your checkout cart, online store, affiliates, and email marketing so you don’t have to connect other tools.

How much does Podia cost?

Podia has two plans, Mover and Shaker. Here’s a quick look at Podia’s pricing, but you can learn more about what’s in each plan on the pricing page.

Mover: $33/month paid annually ($39/month paid monthly), 5% transaction fee: Includes your website, blog, landing pages, unlimited products, courses, and email marketing Shaker: $75/month paid annually ($89/month paid monthly), no transaction fees: Includes everything in Mover plus affiliates

Podia also has a free 30-day trial where you can test all features before signing up.

What plans include free migrations?

Migrations of products and customers are completely free when you sign up for a Mover or Shaker plan. Mover plans include free migration for 20 products and Shaker plans include free migration for 30 products.

You can also get free email list migrations with any paid Podia Email plan.

How do product and course migrations work?

The number of products we can migrate depends on your plan. - Mover: 20 products - Shaker: 30 products Here's what we can migrate for you: -Online course content, including files and text -Digital downloads, including files and text -All online course and digital download customer email addresses -All free community/membership subscribers

If you need more products imported, send us a message at hello@podia.com to discuss your options.

How do email migrations work?

If you’re buying a paid Podia Email plan, we’ll help you migrate your email lists, tags, segments, and campaigns from your existing email service provider (ESP) to Podia for free.

With your Podia Email plan, we’ll migrate: - Subscribers - Tags - Segments - Up to 5 email templates* - Up to 10 email campaigns Data migrated for email subscribers is limited to: First and Last name, email address, and associated tags. We cannot store or migrate additional information such as mailing addresses, phone numbers, conditional splits, or subscriber notes.

Every platform is different so it’s unlikely that you will end up with a setup that’s exactly the same as your existing ESP – but we can get you pretty close! *Likewise, templates will be recreated in Podia using the design features available. Some fonts and formatting will change to adapt to our editor.

You can learn more about email migrations here.

How long will my migration take?

The length of your migration will depend on what platform you are coming from and the number of files your products contain. If you’d like an estimate before signing up, just click the support button at the bottom right of this page or email hello@podia.com with the subject “Migration estimate”. You can find more information on the migration process in this support article.

Can I pay you to build my website for me?

While we don’t offer an official website-building service, we can recommend people who do. The Podia Pros are expert designers, specialists, and educators who know Podia inside and out.

Whether you need a done-for-you service or some fresh new ideas, the Podia Pros are here to help with web design services, admin assistance, course and product setup, and consultations. Learn more about hiring a Podia Pro here.
